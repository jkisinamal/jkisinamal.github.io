Eye of the Tiger

**The eye of the tiger is a symbol of strength and courage. It is a reminder that you are capable of anything you set your mind to. You can overcome any obstacle that comes your way. You have the power to achieve your dreams and goals.

God and Country

A Helping Hand

And do not forget to do good and to share with others (the weak, the elderly, the orphan, the unborn and anyone that is helpless – my words), for with such sacrifices God is pleased. cf Hebrews 13:16

Charity & Liberty

**Charity and liberty are the foundation of a free society. It is the duty of every citizen to help those in need and to protect the rights of all people. When we work together to build a better world, we can achieve great things.

The Light of the Lamp

**The light of the LAMP is a symbol of knowledge and wisdom. It is a reminder that education is the key to success. When we learn new things and share our knowledge with others, we can change the world for the better.

Dreams of a Baker

**Dreams of a baker is a symbol of hope and perseverance. It is a reminder that no matter how difficult life may be, there is always a way to achieve your dreams. When we work hard and never give up, we can accomplish anything we set our minds to.

Jars of Clay

“But we have this treasure in earthen vessels, that the excellency of the power may be of God, and not of us.” (KJV: 2 Corinthians 4: 7) Pastors are like JARS OF CLAY. Designed to serve in the most unique and special calling in spite of the fact of their vulnerability. The One who called made them extraordinaire.

From High Throne down to the Cross

This original piece expresses the Four Faces of Christ as given in the revealed GOSPELS (King, Servant, Man, Divine). Cf. Philippians 2:5-11

Victory Over Sorrow

**Victory over sorrow is a symbol of hope and healing. It is a reminder that no matter how difficult life may be, there is always a way to overcome our pain and suffering. When we have faith and trust in God, we can find peace and joy in the midst of our trials.
